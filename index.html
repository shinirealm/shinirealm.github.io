Rules of the Shinigami Realm

By being in this server, you agree to these rules. No excuses.

⚠ No Doxxing or Sharing Personal Info

Do NOT share real names, IPs, DMs, images, or anything personal. Zero tolerance.

🚫 Keep Trash Talk Light – No Extreme Toxicity

Jokes are fine. Threats, hate, and attacks are not. Respect people.

🚭 No Discrimination or Hate Speech

No racism, sexism, slurs, homophobia, etc. Not even as a “joke.”

🇬🇧 English Only in Public Channels

Speak English in all public channels for clarity. DM in other languages if needed.

👮 Respect the Staff Team

Do NOT argue with, troll, or ignore staff. Calm appeals only.

🔞 No NSFW or Inappropriate Content

No porn, gore, explicit content. Keep the Realm clean.

🚫 No Cheats, Hacks, Macros, or Exploits

Anyone caught using unfair advantages will be banned instantly.

💬 No Spamming, Flooding, or Trolling

No message spam, emoji floods, mic spam, or baiting.

📣 No Advertising Without Permission

No promos, invites, or links to other Discords or socials.

📌 Use Channels for Their Purpose

Don’t misuse strategy chats for memes or off-topic convos.

👤 One Account Per Person

No alts or backups unless approved.

📝 Don’t Impersonate Staff or High Ranks

Don’t fake names, avatars, or tags. Instant ban.

🎮 Game Integrity Is Everything

No griefing, sabotaging, or throwing.

🚶 No Begging for Ranks/Roles

Earn ranks through effort, not begging.

💬 No Toxic Private Messaging

Harassment in DMs related to the server is also a rule break.

🚫 Keep Drama Out of the Realm

This is a gaming space, not your venting zone.

⏰ Don’t Leak Internal Info

Breaking internal trust = instant removal.

🕐 Respect Time Zones & Pings

Don’t spam ping or ignore others’ time zones.

🎤 Voice Chat Rules Apply

No loud music, screaming, or trolling in VC.

💪 Represent Shinigami Realm with Honor

You represent the Realm even outside. Act elite, stay elite.

📝 We Follow Discord & Roblox ToS

Breaking either = punishment or ban. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse.

Follow them... or be cast into the void.
